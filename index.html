About Me

I am Kadir Toptaş, a student at Yıldız Technical University and a passionate game developer. My fascination with video games has been an integral part of my life since I was 5 years old, and I have decided to turn this passion into a professional career.

My game development journey began over two years ago with the Unity engine. During this time, I focused on mobile platforms and successfully published two games on the Google Play Store. Alongside these experiences, I have developed numerous demos and prototypes to nurture my creativity and technical skills. Driven by a desire for continuous growth, I have been working with Unreal Engine for the past year, elevating the experience I initially gained with the Blueprint system by significantly improving my proficiency in C++.

With the goal of mastering every stage of the development process, I actively use a wide range of technologies and software:

Game Engines: Unreal Engine, Unity

Programming Languages: C++, C#, Python, SQL, HTML, CSS

3D/2D Design & Texturing: Blender, Maya, 3ds Max, Substance 3D Painter, Aseprite, Photoshop, Illustrator

Video, UI/UX & Audio: Adobe Premiere Pro, After Effects, Figma, FL Studio

Office Software: Microsoft Office

I have participated in numerous Game Jams, Hackathons and Bootcamps to continuously test my theoretical knowledge and practical experience. These events have strengthened my problem-solving and teamwork skills.

With an unceasing desire to learn and improve, I am determined to build a solid career in this field. For me, game development is more than just a job; it is the art of creating new worlds, telling stories, and offering players unforgettable experiences. My greatest ambition is to achieve lasting and innovative work in the industry, driven by this passion.
